EndoMeetReality

A nonprofit devoted to making Endometriosis meet reality.

Endometriosis is a debilitating disease which affects 10-20% of girls and women. The most common symptom is severe pain during periods, resulting in vomitting and missed classes or work.

We're working to reduce the diagnosis gap while increasing awareness and support for sufferers.

On average, it takes 9 years of suffering to receive a diagnosis.

The lack of support and awareness for Endometriosis isn't just tragic--it's literally so disjointed from reality that it's hard to believe.

We work with High Schools to:

  1. Educate and Support HS Nurses
  2. Enhance Health and Sex Ed. Classes
  3. Analyze Attendance Data to Identify Sufferers
  4. Support teachers with Endo Students in their Classes
  5. Offer Seminars and Conferences for Families
